Preface. . .

When the Prince/Princess in the Kingdom of Laecan is of age, a tournment called the Trails of the Heart is held, often times when a royal can't decide or has too many tutors. Or if they wish to marry someone that isn't of royal blood as a way for their suitor to prove themselves

Cosmina Millington

The sole heir to the Millington throne and soon to be queen of Laecan. Renowed for her beauty and kindness, she is beloved by all. Though despite her protests and infamous temper, the Trails of the Heart are the only way she can be with her beloved knight.

Merhild 'the Nobel' Allencourt

Merhild is the only daughter in her family to choose knighthood over being married off. As such, she ascended on her own, earning every accolade despite dissapointing her parents. Though she was prepared to live a live in service to the throne, she too becam eone of many to fall for Princess Cosmina, the only difference is the Princess' reciopcial feelings.

Lala the Court Jester

Lala the Court Jester is a desendant of a long line of dark witches, cursed with immortality by her mother in order to avenge her and her coven.Forever trying to infilrate the royal court to do so, and finally succeding. Afterall, who would suspect a fool?
